Hello, ScummVM!

May 12, 2020
Hi! I’m Dylan Servilla, and this summer I’m going to be working on ScummVM’s Macromedia Director engine as a GSoC student. Director was used to create a ton of multimedia from the late ’80s to early ’00s, and I think it’s important that we save this digital history from software rot. In particular, I hope to help the engine reach full support for Spaceship Warlock and The Journeyman Project in the near future.…
